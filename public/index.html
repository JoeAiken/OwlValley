About Owl Valley

We are a family owned hop yard and vegetable farm located in the Adirondacks of Upstate New York. Awarded Warren County Farm of the Year in 2016 we are a one of a kind operation ranging from our two acre hop yard to our 30x72 ft greenhouse.
